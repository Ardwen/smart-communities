About us

The SRTI Lab is working with the Urbana, Champaign and Savoy communities to help them analyze their maintenance request data and test new technologies for use in the community. For Champaign, this has included creating geo visualizations of the data as pictured above and starting work on correlating maintenance issues like flooding to weather data for the same timeframes. We’re also testing AI technologies like video recognition to streamline workflows. We’ve begun investigating areas of opportunity where we could partner with Urbana and Savoy as well.
